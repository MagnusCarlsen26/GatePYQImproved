ANS: B,D

During the connection establishment phase:

  • The client sends a segment with the SYN = 1 flag to request a connection from the server.

  • The server responds with both ACK = 1 (to acknowledge the client‚Äôs request) and SYN = 1 (to send its own connection request), i.e., SYN + ACK .

  • The client then sends the final ACK in the third step of the handshake
